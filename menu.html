Main Dish

Jajangmyeon

₩ 3500

Jajangmyeon is a popular Korean dish featuring thick wheat noodles served with a flavorful and dark jajang sauce made from black bean paste, stir-fried with diced pork or beef, onions, and vegetables, providing a satisfyingly chewy texture and a well-balanced combination of savory and slightly sweet flavors.

Tteokbokki

₩ 5000

Tteokbokki is a popular Korean street food dish consisting of chewy rice cakes stir-fried in a spicy and savory sauce. It is often accompanied by fish cakes, vegetables, and a rich red pepper paste. Tteokbokki offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors, providing a satisfying and spicy culinary experience.

Naengmyeon

₩ 12000

Naengmyeon is a refreshing Korean noodle dish traditionally served cold. It features long, thin buckwheat noodles in a tangy broth, garnished with cucumber, radish, and a boiled egg. Naengmyeon offers a cooling sensation and is perfect for hot summer days, providing a unique blend of flavors and textures.

Bibimbap

₩ 13000

Bibimbap is a popular Korean rice dish that combines an assortment of colorful vegetables, marinated meat, and a fried egg on top of a bed of steamed rice. It's served with spicy gochujang sauce on the side, allowing you to mix and enjoy a harmonious blend of flavors and textures in each bite.

Bulgogi Deopbap

₩ 25000

Bulgogi Deopbap is a delicious Korean dish featuring tender, marinated beef cooked to perfection and served over a bed of steamed rice. The beef is thinly sliced, marinated in a savory blend of soy sauce, garlic, and other seasonings, creating a rich and flavorful meal that is both satisfying and delightful.

Kimchi Jjigae

₩ 10000

Kimchi Jjigae is a spicy and hearty Korean stew made with fermented cabbage, pork, tofu, and various vegetables. Packed with bold flavors, this comforting dish is known for its tangy and spicy taste. It's a perfect combination of fermented goodness and satisfying ingredients that will warm you up with every spoonful.

Desserts

Hotteok

₩ 2000

Hotteok is a popular Korean street food known for its delightful taste. It features a fluffy pancake filled with a sweet mixture of cinnamon, brown sugar, and chopped nuts. The pancake is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, creating a delicious contrast of textures. Enjoy this warm and satisfying treat!

Yakgwa

₩ 6500

Yakgwa is a traditional Korean sweet pastry made with honey, sesame oil, and wheat flour. It is shaped into small bite-sized pieces and deep-fried until golden. Yakgwa has a crispy exterior and a chewy, sweet center, often flavored with hints of ginger and cinnamon. It is commonly enjoyed during festive occasions.

Patbingsoo

₩ 8000

Patbingsoo, also known as Korean shaved ice dessert, is a popular Korean dessert made with finely shaved ice topped with sweet red bean paste, fruits, condensed milk, and various toppings. It offers a refreshing and indulgent treat, perfect for cooling down during hot summer days in Korea. it's a nice choice for summer.

Bunggeopang

₩ 3500

Bungeoppang is a popular Korean street food that consists of a fish-shaped pastry filled with sweet red bean paste. It has a crispy exterior and a warm, gooey center. Bungeoppang is a delicious treat enjoyed by locals and tourists alike, especially during the winter season.

Gyeongdan

₩ 1500

Gyeongdan is a traditional Korean rice cake made from glutinous rice flour. It is typically small and round, coated with various ingredients like sesame seeds or bean powder. Gyeongdan is known for its chewy texture and is often enjoyed as a sweet snack or dessert during festive occasions.

Beverages

Makgeolli

₩ 4500

Makgeolli is a traditional Korean rice wine with a slightly sweet and tangy flavor. It is milky-white in appearance and has a smooth, refreshing taste. Made from fermented rice, makgeolli is a popular alcoholic beverage in Korea, enjoyed for its unique flavor and cultural significance.

Soju

₩ 3000

Soju is a clear, distilled alcoholic beverage that originated in Korea. It is traditionally made from rice, but can also be produced from other grains like barley or sweet potatoes. Soju has a clean taste and smooth with a hint of sweetness, popular choice for social gatherings in Korea.

Sikhye

₩ 2500

Sikhye is a traditional Korean sweet rice beverage. It is made by fermenting cooked rice with malt barley and adding sugar for sweetness. Sikhye has a refreshing and slightly sweet flavor with a hint of malt. It is often served chilled and enjoyed as a dessert or a refreshing drink in Korea.
